------ SAM HART

Sam Hart is a scientist, publisher, and curator living in New York. As a bioinformatician at the Sloan Kettering Institute, Sam works across cancer genomics and cellular engineering. He is Founder and Editor-in-Chief of Avant.org, a distributed project space for research and practice, as well as cutator of the online technical catalog, Research Tactics.
