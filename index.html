Sam Hart is a scientist, publisher, and artist whose work addresses multivalent systems and their responsible engagement. Sam works as a Bioinformatician at Sloan Kettering working between cancer genomics and cellular engineering. He is also Founder and Editor of Avant.org, an online artists' magazine for research and practice.

LABORATORY

Current research in the laboratory of Michel Sadelain and the Center for Cell Engineering focuses on computational methods for developing chimeric antigen receptor immunotherapy.
